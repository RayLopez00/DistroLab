#RayLopez00

1. Research which distro fits your needs.

2. Download the latest image of said Distro.

3. Download Etcher from Etcher.io

4.  Find your boot-able device on Etcher if it is not already found.

5. Find the Image of the Distro you chose.

6. Click flash and wait for it to flash until its complete.

7. Reset your PC.

8. To enter the BIOS hold F2 or F12 or the Delete Key while the PC is Booting

9. When your in the BIOS make the main boot device your chosen removable device.

10. Save the settings and exit.

11. Your PC should reboot to the right OS.
